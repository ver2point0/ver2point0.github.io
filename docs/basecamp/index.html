Why do you want to intern at Basecamp

Basecamp

I want to intern at Basecamp because I want to learn at a company that gets things done with talented people. I feel like I am becoming stagnant in my learning. I keep hitting walls in gaining more practical experience in programming and making progress towards depth of knowledge not just breadth. More so, I think this internship will highlight areas in which I can improve and build upon my strengths. What I would really like is a concrete and focused path to push me to be better than I am now. This is where mentorship comes into play. I believe that it is one of the key factors that separates the average from the great. Another reason I want to intern this Summer at Basecamp is because it is remote friendly. I love the idea that I will be able to talk to my future mentor on a sunny Alaskan afternoon. Lastly, I am super EXCITED to work on real projects with real impact! These, too, can only help me become better. Nathan, Michelle, and Danny are happy with their results. I will be too! :)
